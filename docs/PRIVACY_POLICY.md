**Portfolio Data:**

- Risk metrics and portfolio configurations

**Communications:**
- Support requests and customer service interactions
- Feedback, surveys, and feature requests

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data:**
- Pages viewed, time spent on pages, and navigation patterns
- Browser type, operating system, and device information
- IP address and general geographic location
- Search queries within the Service

**Cookies and Tracking:**
- Session cookies for authentication and preferences
- Analytics cookies through Google Analytics
- Third-party integrations (Stripe, Vercel)

**Service Interactions:**
- API calls made through the Service (Alpha Vantage, Gemini AI, Yahoo Finance)
- Cache metrics and performance data
- Error logs and diagnostic information

### 2.3 Third-Party Information

We receive limited information from:
- Stripe (subscription status, billing history)
- Authentication providers (Google OAuth)
- Data providers (Alpha Vantage, Yahoo Finance)

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, we process personal data based on:
- **Consent:** When you create an account or subscribe
- **Contract:** To fulfill our service obligations
- **Legitimate Interests:** Service improvement, fraud prevention, security
- **Legal Compliance:** Regulatory requirements

You have the right to withdraw consent at any time without affecting past processing.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share information with:

**Service Providers:**
- **Stripe:** For payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)

- **Google:** For analytics and OAuth (Google Analytics privacy policy applies)
- **Supabase:** For database and authentication
- **Upstash:** For caching and rate limiting

**API Providers:**
- **Alpha Vantage:** Stock price data
- **Yahoo Finance:** Financial data
- **Gemini AI:** Powered AI analysis

**Legal Requirements:**
- Law enforcement when required by law
- Court orders or legal process
- Protection of rights, privacy, or safety

**Business Transitions:**
- In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with notice)

## 6. Data Retention

**Active Accounts:**
- Account data retained as long as your account is active
- Portfolio data retained indefinitely (unless deleted by you)
- Chat history: Free tier 14 days, Basic tier 1 year, Premium tier indefinitely

**After Deletion:**
- Account deletion removes most personal data within 30 days
- Aggregated analytics data retained indefinitely
- Backups may retain data for up to 90 days
- Legal holds may retain data longer if required

**Payment Data:**
- Retained per Stripe's retention policy (typically 7 years for tax/compliance)
- You may request deletion except where legally required to retain

## 10. Security

We implement:
- SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
- Rate limiting and DDoS protection
- Circuit breakers for API failures
- Regular security audits and monitoring
- Principle of least privilege for data access
- PCI-DSS compliance for payment data

**Note:** No method is 100% secure. Unauthorized access remains possible.

## 11. Data Breaches

In the event of a data breach, we will:
1. Investigate the scope and nature of the breach
2. Notify affected users without undue delay (typically within 72 hours for GDPR violations)
3. Provide information about the breach and steps you can take
4. Notify relevant authorities where legally required
5. Cooperate with law enforcement
